Light and fluffy popcorn seasoned with your choice of flavors.

üìù Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup popcorn kernels
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional seasonings: butter, cheese powder, or caramel

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add a few popcorn kernels to the pot and cover.
  3. Once the kernels pop, add the rest of the popcorn kernels in an even layer.
  4. Cover the pot and remove from heat for 30 seconds.
  5. Return the pot to heat and gently shake it as the popcorn pops.
  6. Once popping slows, remove from heat and season with salt and your choice of toppings.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tip: For a sweet treat, drizzle with melted chocolate!

A delightful fruit tart with a buttery crust, creamy filling, and topped with fresh seasonal fruits.

üìù Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and diced
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons cold water
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Assorted fresh fruits (e.g., strawberries, kiwi, blueberries)

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and salt. Cut in butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Stir in egg yolk and cold water until the dough comes together. Press into a tart pan.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes or until golden brown. Let cool.
  5. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla until stiff peaks form.
  6. Spread the cream mixture over the cooled crust.
  7. Arrange fresh fruits on top of the cream.
  8. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.

Tip: Brush the fruits with a light glaze of apricot jam for extra shine!

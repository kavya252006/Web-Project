Creamy cheesecake with a buttery graham cracker crust and a hint of vanilla.

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs

  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and melted butter in a bowl. Press into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese, 1 1/4 cups sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing on low speed until just blended.
  5. Pour the filling over the crust.
  6. Bake for 55-60 minutes or until the center is set. Cool completely before removing from the pan.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.

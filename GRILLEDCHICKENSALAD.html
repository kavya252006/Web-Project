Grilled Chicken Salad

A refreshing salad with grilled chicken, fresh greens, cherry tomatoes, and a tangy vinaigrette.

üìù Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vinaigrette dressing of choice

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush chicken breasts with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill chicken for 5-6 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.
  4. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes, then slice it thinly.
  5. In a large bowl, combine salad greens, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and feta cheese.
  6. Add the sliced chicken on top.
  7. Drizzle with vinaigrette dressing and toss gently to combine.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tip: Add avocado slices for extra creaminess!

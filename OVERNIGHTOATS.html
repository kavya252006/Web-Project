Creamy oats soaked overnight with yogurt and topped with fresh fruits, nuts, and a drizzle of honey.

  • Fresh fruits (e.g., berries, banana slices)
  • Nuts (e.g., almonds, walnuts)
  • Honey or maple syrup for drizzling

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions:

  1. In a jar or bowl, combine oats, milk, yogurt, and chia seeds. Stir well.

  4. Top with fresh fruits, nuts, and a drizzle of honey or maple syrup.
